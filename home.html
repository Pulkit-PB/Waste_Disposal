How It Works

Our platform helps you dispose of menstrual waste safely and sustainably: Find a Collection Bin: Use our map to locate the nearest collection bin in your area. Book a Pickup: For convenience, book a door-to-door waste pickup service. Dispose Properly: Drop off sanitary products at a nearby bin or schedule a pickup, ensuring your waste is treated and disposed of safely. Track and Earn: Track your disposal history and earn reward points for responsible waste disposal, which you can redeem for eco-friendly products.











About Us

We are dedicated to solving India's menstrual waste problem. Our mission is to provide easy and eco-friendly disposal solutions to help reduce the environmental impact of sanitary waste. By combining technology with sustainability, we empower women to make waste disposal safer, simpler, and more responsible. Together, we can protect the planet while maintaining hygiene.
